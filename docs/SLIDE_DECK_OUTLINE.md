# Slide Deck Outline

For the final detailed internal and pitch deck, see [FINAL_TEAM_PITCH_DECK.md](FINAL_TEAM_PITCH_DECK.md).

## 1. Title

Anirvium AI: Trajectory Intelligence for Enterprise Support Agents.

## 2. Problem

AI support agents are entering high-stakes workflows, but teams lack observability, policy assurance, evidence tracking, and improvement loops.

## 3. Solution

Anirvium AI records, evaluates, diagnoses, and optimizes every support-agent trajectory.

## 4. Why Now

Enterprises are scaling AI agents faster than governance and QA systems. Agent failures now create SLA, trust, refund, security, and compliance risk.

## 5. Product Demo

Show the support queue, run console, final actions, safe drafts, approval states, and evidence IDs.

## 6. Technical Architecture

FastAPI backend, React dashboard, multi-agent runner, synthetic data, trajectory logger, deterministic evaluator, AMD-ready OpenAI-compatible LLM client.

## 7. Trajectory Intelligence Engine

Explain span logging, graph visualization, evidence grounding, policy checks, diagnosis, and optimizer recommendations.

## 8. AMD Compute Usage

vLLM/ROCm on AMD Developer Cloud, benchmark script, tokens/sec, latency, throughput, ticket count, agent-step count, trajectory score, token efficiency.

## 9. Market Opportunity

Every enterprise deploying AI support agents needs observability, evals, governance, and continuous optimization.

## 10. Roadmap

LLM-as-judge, human approval UI, hosted deployment, SQLite history, model routing, prompt regression benchmarks, enterprise integrations.
