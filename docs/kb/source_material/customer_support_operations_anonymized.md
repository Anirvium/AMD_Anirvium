What is USDT? 
In simple words, the United States Dollar Tether (USDT)  is the first and most popular stablecoin on the crypto market, it is a crypto token with a fixed price tied to the American dollar course (1 USD = 1 USDT).

Tether (USDT) is a blockchain-based cryptocurrency whose tokens in circulation are backed by an equivalent amount of U.S. dollars, making it a stablecoin with a price pegged to USD $1.00. Stablecoins track traditional fiat currencies, like the dollar, the euro, or the Japanese yen, which are held in a designated bank account.

 What is a stablecoin? 
Stablecoin is a cryptocurrency with a stable course that is not exposed to market volatility, unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others.
Will the USDT be stored in the platform? 
No, the platform does not store USDT and does not exchange crypto for fiat currencies. This is done only by authorized and licensed crypto exchanges and wallets in which the client’s money is stored.

These are financial institutions licensed to operate with cryptocurrencies and are located in Europe and Asia. Unfortunately, we will not disclose any other details.

New payment method verification 
New payment method verification is triggered when a client makes a deposit on the platform with a payment method that was not verified before. The customer will have 14 days to verify the account without restrictions, then it will be restricted until verification is completed.

	•	The customer completed mandatory verification, or is undergoing mandatory verification, and
	•	Makes a cumulative deposit of, ≥1000 USD with a new payment method.

The documents required for this verification are: 
	1.	Proof of identity
	2.	Proof of address
	3.	3-d selfie
	4.	Proof of payment (of the payment method/s used to make a cumulative deposit of ≥1000 USD).

Example:
A verified client makes a deposit of 100 USD (less than 1000 USD) with a new payment method. The verification of this payment method will not be requested. However, if the user makes another deposit of 900 USD with this payment method, the cumulative amount reaches 1000 USD. In this case, verification will be requested.

This verification reason is activated when a client makes deposits that sum up to 5000 USD within 60 days. The customer will have 14 days to verify the account without restrictions, then it will be restricted until verification is completed.

Refund rules for accounts blocked due to a violation 


The platform only refunds the leftover deposit money if it was not withdrawn or lost in trading. Profits are not included in the refund. Also, please note that for repeated violations, there won't be any refunds. 
Example: A client deposits $100. After trading, he increases his balance up to $200 and withdraws $50. The balance by the time of blocking is $150. After the account is blocked, he will be refunded $50 only. The remaining $100 is retained. 
Formula: [Deposit - Withdrawals] (If there is a positive balance by the time of blocking)

What are bonuses? 
Bonuses are not-real funds, they are a financial cushion that allows customers to trade in FTT after the real deposited funds are depleted. When a customer uses bonuses to open a deal, and the deal is successful, the profit is credited as real money in the balance. Here are a few things to note regarding bonuses: 
	•	Bonuses can only be used if ALL the real balance is used in trading, or if the available real funds are not sufficient to open a deal (less than $1). 
	•	When customers request a withdrawal, all bonuses are deducted from the account. 
	•	Users can only withdraw the profit earned using bonuses, but not the bonus itself. 
	•	If the customer is not active for 180 days and there are bonuses in the account, the bonuses are deducted as part of the inactivity fee. 

How to obtain Bonuses? 
Customers can receive bonuses by applying promo codes. The only promo code that grants bonuses available at the moment is 'Bonus On Pay.'
