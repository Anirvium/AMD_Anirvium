Bonus FAQ

What is a Deposit Bonus? 💰
Deposit bonuses are virtual funds added to our customers' balances, giving them extra funds to trade with. Bonuses are credited to the account as a percentage of the customer’s deposit (e.g., 50% of the deposit) and are available immediately for trading when the deposit reflects in the account. 
Customers can get a bonus by applying a promo code when depositing. Promo codes are in the 'Choose Promocode' section in the Deposit tab or on our social media. Each bonus promo code is linked to a bonus offer, unique to the customer's account. Bonuses are available to new and active users under special conditions; however, only one offer can be active simultaneously, even with multiple sub-accounts. 
After receiving the bonus, users are assigned to reach a specific turnover target amount. Once a user completes the task, a real-funds reward is credited to the account. 
How does it work? 📗
When a bonus offer is active, the deposit (real) and bonus parts of a user's balance are used in trades proportionally to the final ratio of bonuses/real funds after the deposit. 
For example 
	•	The customer has no balance. 
	•	Deposit made: $100
	•	Bonus received: $50 (50% of the deposit). 
	•	Total balance: $150. 
Real funds represent 67% of the total balance, while bonuses represent 33%. Thus, the ratio of funds is 67/33. 
Each trade will consist of 67% real funds and 33% bonuses. Profits and losses consist of the same proportion. 
✅ Successful trade (FTT)
	•	FTT trade of $10 with 90% profitability.
	•	Profit: $9 ($6.03 will be added to real balance; $2.97 will be added to bonus balance)
❌ Unsuccessful trade 
	•	Flex/FTT trade of $10 with 90% profitability
	•	Loss: $10 ($6.7 will be deducted from real balance; $3.3 will be deducted from bonus balance)
🛑 Cancelled trade
	•	FTT trade of $10; Cancelled with a balance adjustment of $8. 
	•	cancellation adjustment: $8 ($2.64 will be deducted from bonus balance; $5.36 will be deducted from real balance)

Reward 🎁
To withdraw the reward, users must complete the turnover task goal. The reward amount is fixed, and the user is informed when the bonus is received. Only FTT/Flex trades on the sub-account with bonuses count towards meeting the task. Users can track their progress in the 'Rewards’ tab, where they will find a progress bar. 
Once the turnover task is completed, the reward amount becomes available for withdrawal. It's important to note that this is not a withdrawal of the bonus funds. The bonus cannot be withdrawn at any point— what you can withdraw is a fixed real amount defined as the reward, which was communicated when the bonus was granted. 
🔢 How is the reward calculated? 
The reward is calculated based on a multiplier. The multiplier is determined by the amount of the first deposit, geo, the source of the user registration, and completion of other bonus offers. All users start with an x1 multiplier (cap). Here's the formula: Cap × Bonus % × Deposit
For example 👉🏼 User deposited $10 for the first time with a 50% bonus offer. Reward = 1 × 50% (0.5) × $10 = $5.
After receiving the reward, the bonus balance resets to zero, even if the bonus balance > reward.

Deactivation of a Bonus Offer 🛑
A bonus offer can be deactivated, removing all bonus funds from the accounts. This can happen in the following scenarios:
	•	Voluntary deactivation → The user cancels the offer manually in the 'Tasks & Rewards' section of the 'Rewards’ tab by selecting 'Decline Bonus.’
	•	Forced deactivation -> Triggered by expiration or by system decision.
	•	Withdrawal request → Any withdrawal request from the bonus account will cancel all bonuses. 
	•	Internal Transfer request → Any transfer between sub-accounts with bonuses will cancel all bonuses. 
	•	Turnover task Completion – The offer is deactivated once the required turnover is achieved.
Special Situations ⚠️
New deposits to an account with an active bonus
When users make a new deposit to an account with an active bonus, the total balance increases, the bonus amount remains unchanged, and the proportion/ratio to open trades remains unchanged, as it is fixed at the initial deposit with the bonus.
For example 
	•	The customer has no balance. 
	•	Deposit made: $100
	•	Bonus received: $50 (50% of the deposit). 
	•	Total balance: $150. 
	•	The ratio of funds is 67/33. 
After an additional deposit of $30:
	•	New deposit: $30
	•	Total balance: $180
	•	Real funds: $130
	•	Bonus: $50 (unchanged)
The new proportion of real/bonus is 72/28%. Despite the change in the total proportion of funds on the account, all trades will be opened in the original proportion of 67/33%.
Bonus and real balance = 0 
If the bonus balance reaches 0 due to trading activity (not cancellation of the offer), the offer remains active. The user can still complete the turnover task using only real funds. 
Users can: 
	•	Make a new deposit to top up their balance
	•	Continue trading to reach the required trading turnover (target amount)
	•	Complete the task and receive a Reward.
Not enough balance to maintain the ratio
When users do not have enough bonus funds to cover the ratio, the system covers it with real funds. The actual ratio used to open the trade (based on real and bonus funds used in that trade) determines the profit/loss split.
When users do not have enough real funds to cover the ratio, they can only open trades that respect the original ratio. To continue trading with higher amounts, users can make an additional deposit to increase their real fund balance.

	•	Bonus = $5 (50% of the deposit)
	•	Balance = $15 ($10 real + $5 bonus)

You can use it to explore the new bonus mechanics and complete the trading task to unlock a real-fund reward. Please note that this bonus does not require an immediate deposit if you have available funds in your balance.
Can I receive a bonus on a crypto account?
Yes! If a bonus offer is available, you can receive it on a crypto account. It works the same way as on any other account.
Can I make purchases or use features while my bonus is active?
Yes! With an active bonus, you can still shop in the Marketplace or use other platform features. Purchases will be deducted from your real account. You can also use your other sub-accounts to manage and use the subscriptions.
I preferred the previous bonus system. Can I still use it?
We understand that the new bonus format may take some time to get used to. Just to clarify, the platform has fully transitioned to this new system, so the previous one is no longer in use. The new format is designed to give customers a clear reward for completing tasks, and all active bonuses now come with a defined trading goal and a guaranteed real-fund reward. If this system doesn’t suit your preferences, you’re always free to trade without using a bonus. We’re also happy to pass along your feedback to the responsible team, as we’re constantly working to improve the platform based on our users’ experience.
What reward will I receive after completing my turnover task?
The reward amount you receive depends on the specific bonus offer.
	•	If the offer is already activated, you can see the exact reward in your account by going to the Rewards tab and checking the Tasks & Rewards section.
	•	If the offer is not yet activated, the system cannot display the exact reward. However, in most cases, the reward is equal to or greater than your deposit bonus.
For example, if you receive a $50 bonus, your reward may be $50 or more. Once the offer is activated, the system will immediately show both the reward amount and the turnover requirement.
I made a profit, but part of it went to my bonus balance. Can I get that refunded? 
In the current bonus system, trades use both real and bonus funds based on a fixed ratio set when the bonus offer is applied. This same ratio is used to split profits from successful trades. You don’t lose your real money, your share of the profit stays in your real balance and can be withdrawn. The portion earned using bonus funds goes to your bonus balance and can’t be withdrawn. Since this split is based on how the trade was funded, we’re unable to refund the bonus part of the profit. If you prefer not to use bonus funds in trades, you can cancel the bonus offer and continue trading with real funds only.
I didn’t receive my reward or see a notification. What should I do?
You can go to the Profile section and tap the bell icon to check your Notifications, or check your Transaction History to see if the reward was credited.

Why are bonuses given / what’s the benefit to the user?
The new bonus system gives you extra ways to trade and explore different opportunities. When bonuses are used, they increase overall trading activity on the platform, which allows us to keep adding rewards, improving features, and running promotions. In turn, you get even more chances to trade and benefit in the future.
Why is the turnover high, and why not reduce it?
The bonus task is intended as an incentive reward, so it needs to strike a balance between being challenging and achievable. We’ve set the turnover at a level that gives you a fair chance to complete it while ensuring the bonus remains a meaningful reward for sustained trading activity.
